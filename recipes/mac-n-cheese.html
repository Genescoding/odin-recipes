Mac N' Cheese

The taste of childhood...

Mac N' Cheese has always been a staple while I grew up, which then became a comfort dish as I got older, and since I make it frequently, It's important to keep it simple. In the following recipe, we will combine common household Ingredients in the most delicious way known to mankind.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Chopped garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook 60g of dry Macaroni in boiled water for 7 minutes, then drain the water accordingly.
  2. Add a stick of butter to a heated pan, then add chopped garlic.
  3. Add 1 cup of milk and whisk gently before adding the cooked Macaroni.
  4. Add 50g of shredded cheddar and whisk until the desired creaminess is achieved.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and parsley(optional) to taste.
